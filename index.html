Capture from anywhere

Install Coyote and it appears in your phone's Share menu. Hit Share on any signup page → pick Coyote → the form opens with the URL and page title pre-filled.

1
Install Coyote

Click the ⬇ Install button at the top of this page. On iPhone, tap Share in Safari, then "Add to Home Screen."

2
Sign up for a free trial

Use any service you want to try — Hulu, Netflix, Notion, anything.

3
Share the page to Coyote

Tap the Share icon (the square with the up-arrow on iPhone, or the three-dot menu → Share on Android/desktop). Pick Coyote from the list.

4
Add cost + end date

Coyote opens with the URL and page title pre-filled. You only need to enter the monthly cost and trial end date, then save.

What if I'm on desktop?

On Mac or Windows, install Coyote as a desktop app (the ⬇ Install button at the top works in Chrome and Edge). The installed app pins to your dock or taskbar. When you finish a signup, you can copy the URL and use the + Add subscription button manually — takes about ten seconds total.

Coyote also remembers everything across devices automatically when you use the same browser.
